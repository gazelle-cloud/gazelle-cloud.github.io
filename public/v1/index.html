      Welcome to Gazelle

      Streamline Azure Landing Zones

      Gazelle is your ultimate toolkit for creating, managing, and experimenting with Azure landing zones. Designed for teams who value precision and efficiency, Gazelle adapts to the specific needs of each tenant, providing a tailored approach to cloud infrastructure.

      Whether you’re experimenting with configurations, building a scalable Azure platform, or empowering application teams with self-service tools, Gazelle is your reliable partner in the journey.

      Why Gazelle?

      Gazelle stands out with its practical, experiment-driven approach to Azure platform design. It bridges the gap between theory and real-world implementation by offering:

      • A Playground Environment: Experiment safely with landing zones without impacting production systems.
      • Self-Service Capabilities: Empower application teams to create and manage their landing zones independently.
      • Cost Efficiency: Designed for near-zero costs with intelligent budgeting to control expenses.

      Core Features

      1. Isolated Test Environment

      Every update goes through a production-like test environment, ensuring stability and reliability before deployment.

      2. Modular Deployments

      Deployments are divided into focused modules, making them easy to manage, scale, and adapt.

      3. Automated Workflows

      From provisioning to cleanup, Gazelle eliminates manual intervention, ensuring consistency and reducing errors.

      4. Streamlined Processes

      Optimized input/output handling and configuration management keep workflows efficient and predictable.

      Get Started

      Ready to explore Gazelle? Follow the Quick Start Guide to replicate the setup in your Azure tenant. From there, let Gazelle’s automation handle the rest.

      Explore the GitHub Repository to see the codebase and learn more about Gazelle’s configurations.

      Built for Teams Like Yours

      Gazelle isn’t just a platform—it’s a philosophy of clarity, adaptability, and automation. Whether you’re a platform engineer, an application team lead, or a cloud enthusiast, Gazelle equips you with the tools and insights to make your Azure platforms shine.
